About Me

I am Viktoriia

Graphic Designer & Junior Front-End Developer

Based in Bern, Switzerland

I'm a graphic designer with frontend development skills. My passion for design and technology led me to explore both fields, allowing me to create unique and effective projects. Though relatively new to the industry, I've successfully completed several projects and continually refine my skills through learning and practice.

As a graphic designer, I specialize in creating intuitive, visually appealing solutions to help brands stand out, focusing on logos, branding, marketing materials, and social media graphics. Concurrently, I'm an aspiring frontend developer, studying HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create high-quality and responsive websites.

If you need a graphic designer with frontend development potential, please feel free to reach out. I'm eager to work on exciting projects and grow professionally.
