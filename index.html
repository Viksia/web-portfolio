About Me

I am Viktoriia

Graphic Designer & Junior Front-End Developer

Based in Bern, Switzerland

I make sure that the brands and the companies must be aesthetic and well-known. I help companies broadcast love to their customers.

As a beginner front-end developer, I am excited to dive into the world of web development and learn everything I can. I am passionate about creating beautiful, functional and user-friendly websites and applications that can make a difference in people's lives.

I am quick learner and confident in my ability to adapt to new challenges and environments. I possess strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail, which I believe will serve me well in the front-end development world. In short, I am highly motivated to succeed as a front-end developer and am excited to embark on this journey of continuous learning and growth.
